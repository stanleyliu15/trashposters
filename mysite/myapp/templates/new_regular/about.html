Our Mission

In this day and age, environmental issues should be prioritized as we progress as a society. It is not enough to implement top down solutions such as a ban on plastic bags or a tax on unclean vehicles. We want to provide a means for a bottom up solution from within the community and make it easier for people to get involved and make a difference in their environment. Our platform gives citizens the responsibility for the earth in their own hands and work together to keep our planet clean.
